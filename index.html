--- layout: home subtitle: Restoring landscapes and people --- [Checkdam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Check_dam) ~ a simple struture, strongly connected with the earth, that dissipates destructive forces, captures resourses and buffers against floods and drought downstream. We want to connect land stewards with erosion, flood or drought issues with local groups that want to participate in water cycle restoration. Please fill in the form if you want to be involved either way. We are working on a syllabus and facilitator guide to support this work.

Checkdam ~ a simple struture, strongly connected with the earth, that dissipates destructive forces, captures resourses and buffers against floods and drought downstream.

We want to connect land stewards dealing with erosion, flood or drought issues with local groups that want to participate in water cycle restoration. Please fill in the form to register an interest.

We are working on a syllabus and guides for facilitators, land stewards and governing agencies to support this work.
